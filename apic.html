About APIC

The Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients Committee (APIC) is a sector group of the European Chemical Industry Council (CEFIC). APIC represents manufacturers of active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) and works to promote high-quality manufacturing standards and regulatory compliance globally.

Through collaboration with regulatory authorities, international organizations, and the pharmaceutical industry, APIC provides guidance on GMP practices, encourages transparency, and supports global harmonization of API quality standards.

Main Responsibilities

  • Promote and develop GMP standards for API manufacturers worldwide.
  • Represent API industry interests to regulators and international agencies.
  • Provide GMP and regulatory training through conferences and workshops.
  • Publish guidance documents, position papers, and GMP guidelines.
  • Collaborate with organizations such as EMA, ICH, and PIC/S to enhance quality standards.
